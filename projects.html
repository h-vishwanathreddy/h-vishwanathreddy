Nifty 50 Stock Recomendation System

- Automation Anywhere Enterprise Edition, Bot Lab, AARI (Nov 2021 - Jan 2022)

-If an investor is looking for a particular stock to invest in,there goes a fair amount of time spent researching and studying the market trend.
In the present collection of top NIFTY 50 stocks which are fundamentally very strong and can yield the investor good returns in the future it becomes tedious for the investor to frequently look for the data of the stocks in the NSE website.
Here we are building a bot that would account for the parameters such as Open, High, Low, Close, Percentage Change and recommend the best stock to buy/sell at any given point of time all by a single click.
